## Context and Problem Statement

What situation forces a decision? Two or three paragraphs. Written so that a
reader who was not in the room understands why this decision could not be
skipped. For pattern ADRs in this vault: describe the generic scenario, never
a specific engagement.

## Decision Drivers

* Driver 1 (e.g. time-to-first-deployment under a hard deadline)
* Driver 2 (e.g. long-term alignment with the platform vendor's direction)
* Driver 3 (e.g. operability by the team that inherits the system)

* Option B: name
* Option C: name

## Decision Outcome

Chosen option, or for decision-framework ADRs: the recommended default per
context, with the conditions under which each option wins.
